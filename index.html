KNEE ADAPTATION RULE
• Discomfort during adaptation = okay. Sharp pain or swelling = stop. • Lateral bounds and V-cuts: start tiny. Distance and intensity come after the landing mechanics are clean. • Deceleration (Run + Brake) is the most important conditioning drill for long-term knee health on the field.
